There is no strong relationship between latitude and cloudness. However, it is interesting to observe that the cities from -40 deg Latitude to the Northern polar region experience from no-cloud condition to upto 90% cloudness. There is no strong relationship between latitude and wind speed. However, in northern hemispheres there is a flurry of cities with over 20 mph of wind while maximum temperature declines.
